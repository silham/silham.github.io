How to play?

  • Click on OK button and then press enter to start the game.
  • Use the up arrow key to make the runner jump over the fire obstacles
  • Use the down arrow key to make the runner slide under the bird obstacles
  • As you progress through the game, the obstacles will become more challenging and come at you faster
  • Hearts at top right corner indicates runner health. if you hit an obstacle you may loose 1/3 of health
  • Jump and collect hearts to increase runner health
